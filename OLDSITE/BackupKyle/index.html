Welcome to a better medical waste disposal alternative

GIC Medical Disposal is Canada’s premier sharps, pharmaceuticals, and hazardous waste disposal company. The GIC advantage combines superior customer service and environmentally-friendly business conduct with safe and practical solutions that are both convenient and cost-effective; GIC Medical Disposal is dedicated to helping its customers realize cost-efficiencies, reduce risk, and assist in successful operation of any facility. Our compliant, efficient and unparalleled customer-focused solutions are designed to meet the specific requirements of each customer, while fully eliminating any lingering hazmat imprint, or the environmental threat of biomedical waste.

Safe & Easy. Proper handling and disposal of biomedical waste can be a daunting task for healthcare, veterinary and laboratory professionals. Medical waste can be infectious and may harbour organisms with the potentially harmful capacity to self-replicate and spread if not disposed of according to our stringent disposal procedures. GIC Medical Disposal is able to provide its clients with a comprehensive system for the collection, segregation and disposal of hazardous waste. The implementation of our user-friendly systems efficiently disposes and neutralizes your unwanted biomedical waste and hazardous chemicals. The safety of your staff, your patients, the public and the environment are of the utmost importance to us. We advocate and exercise rigid and compliant procedures, which altogether reduce the risk of accident and/or injury while giving you the freedom to effortlessly focus your attention on managing other areas of your business.

Convenient Solutions. At GIC Medical Disposal, we pride ourselves in providing flexible and customer-focused services that are tailored to meet your unique needs – we offer you the freedom and flexibility of choice. Our clients can take advantage of the convenience of our personalized yet comprehensive solutions while enjoying the peace-of-mind that only an industry innovator can deliver. We are able to adapt quickly to the diverse requirements of our clients and design schedules for medical waste pick-up that are accommodating to your business hours. Whether you require a daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, bi-monthly disposal service, we are there to help. Service is also available on a per call basis.

Cost Effective. The implementation of GIC’s solutions can immediately realize user savings through enhanced efficiency. Our team is always available to offer expert advice, smart products and the quality service you expect and deserve. We offer our customers comprehensive solutions that provide for maximum flexibility at the most competitive price the industry has to offer.

Environmentally Friendly. Canada’s provinces and Territories are actively engaged in monitoring and managing the processing and disposal of hazardous waste, primarily under the auspices of their respective environmental ministries. In Ontario, for instance, the Ministry of the Environment (MOE) has initiated a concerted effort to protect the public and the environment from the dangers of hazardous waste through the efforts of the Hazardous Waste Information Network (HWIN). HWIN is part of Canada’s medical waste management network. We can assist you with registering or renewing your generator number. While medical waste companies must adhere to the MOE’s strict regulations, GIC goes the extra mile by implementing extra-stringent, environmentally-focused quality control procedures that further regulate the handling, transportation, storage, treatment and disposal of medical waste. It is both our mission and our profound responsibility to protect the environment and the public from potentially hazardous agents.

Whether you require sharps waste disposal, biomedical waste disposal, laboratory services, hazardous chemical waste disposal or a national pharmaceutical waste and/or recalls, GIC Medical Disposal has a cost-effective turnkey solution for you.
