Sharps Containers

GIC Medical Disposal is committed to reducing needle stick injuries through our smart sharps disposal containers. We have a large assortment of sharps waste disposal containers ranging from 1.5L to 100L, and larger. Whatever your requirements, we have a container that will suit your purposes. We carry environmentally friendly, reusable containers as well as disposable sharps containers. Our environmentally friendly, reusable containers provide our customers with a full service experience while having minimal effects on the environment. The Reusable containers use our state of the art robotic machine to empty out their contents and sterilized. The transportation of disposable sharps containers or reusable sharps container contents must be packaged in accordance with the Ministry Of Environment’s Environmental protection act and regulation 347. GIC’s convenient medical waste pick-up service can be accommodated to your business hours and unique requirements. If one of our customers inadvertently fails to comply with HWIN standards for proper sharps packaging, GIC Medical Disposal will repackage the container free of charge. This guarantees our customers timely and uninterrupted service for medical waste pick-up.

Biomedical Waste Bags

Biomedical waste bags are also utilized in the packaging of sharps containers and medical waste containers for adherence to sharps waste and medical waste pick-up and transportation standards.
