Welcome to our home on the internet

GIC Inc. is Ontario's sharps and pharmaceutical waste company dedicated to providing disposal solutions to meet the needs of our clients.

Safe & Easy. The safety of your staff, your patients and the environment is our priority. Our system is the safest and most environmentally responsible sharps management system.

Convenient Solutions. We pride ourselves in providing flexible services focused on your needs. We offer weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, and by call disposal services.

Cost Effective. GIC's expert advice, smart products and great service can help you reduce costs and increase efficiency. We offer the most comprehensive solutions at competitively priced rates.

Environmentally Friendly. GIC cares about the environment. By reusing our containers instead of throwing them out, a significant reduction to overall waste volumes can be made which helps decrease the impact on our environment.

GIC provides services for a wide array of clients. The majority of our clients fall into the following groups:

  • Hospitals
  • Family Physicians
  • Chiropractors
  • Blood Dontation Clinics
  • Dentists
  • Pharmacies
  • Physio Therapists
  • Acupuncturists
  • Day Clinics
  • Care Homes
  • Surgical Centers
